<p><i>FlickBoard</i> is a flicking-style keyboard, in the style of <a href='https://www.exideas.com/ME/index.php' target='_blank'>MessagEase</a> and <a href='https://github.com/dessalines/thumb-key'>Thumb-Key</a>.</p><p>Type the letters in the center of a key by tapping it. Type the keys in the corners by swiping in that direction.</p>
